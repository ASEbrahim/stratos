StratOS

Strategic Intelligence Operating System

"Tell the system who you are, and it tells you what matters today."

A self-hosted intelligence dashboard that aggregates news, tracks markets, and scores content relevance using a locally fine-tuned AI model. The system improves its own scorer through a distillation loop — Gemini Flash and Claude Opus re-score articles, disagreements become training data, and DoRA fine-tuning updates the local model. Now with an AI agent, rich media feeds, and a 4-mode platform vision.

61,000+ lines · 40+ modules · Solo project · Zero cloud dependency for inference

🧠

Self-Improving Scorer

V2.2 DoRA-tuned Qwen3.5-9B scorer — 40% MAE reduction over V2, zero parse failures. Dual teacher pipeline: Gemini Flash ($6 total) for bulk scoring, Claude Opus for high-precision corrections. 3 generations of training, each cheaper and better.

👤

Profile-Aware Scoring

Same article, different score depending on who's reading. A K-pop agency manager gets 8.5 on idol news; a pipeline welder in Edmonton gets 0.0. The model learned this from 45 profiles across 20+ countries.

📊

Markets + Charts

Real-time candlestick/line/area charts via TradingView with 20 drawing tools, Fibonacci retracement, pattern detection, fullscreen focus mode, and collapsible asset analysis. Per-ticker refresh and side-by-side comparison.

🤖

Strat Agent

Context-aware AI chat with tool use — web search, feed search, watchlist and category management. Fullscreen mode, free/structured chat toggle, suggestion chips, and "Continue in Agent" from any Ask AI response.

📱

Mobile PWA

Installable progressive web app with touch gestures (swipe to save/dismiss, pull-to-refresh, edge swipe navigation), bottom navigation, fullscreen charts, and a two-tier mobile drawing toolbar.

🎨

24 Theme Variants

8 themes (Midnight, Noir, Coffee, Rose, Cosmos, Nebula, Aurora, Sakura) × 3 modes (Normal, Deeper, Brighter). Interactive star canvas with scroll parallax. Theme editor with opacity, glow, and blur sliders.

📰

Rich Media Feeds

Video/stream/image/manga/article detection with grid view. RSS auto-discovery, contextual feed suggestions, Jobs tab with career-focused feeds, and Cloudflare Worker proxy for media sources.

🔐

Multi-User Auth

Email registration with verification codes, bcrypt passwords, 7-day sliding sessions. Per-user data directories with JSONL exports, cross-device sync, and guided onboarding tour.

About
Who I Am

I'm a Computer Engineering student at the American University of Kuwait, graduating Spring 2027. StratOS started because I was tired of opening a dozen tabs every morning — jobs, news, stocks, tech — just to feel caught up. I wanted one place to be "in the know" on everything I care about, ranked by how much it actually matters to me. So I built it.

That "one place" somehow turned into 61,000+ lines of code with a self-improving AI scorer across 3 training generations, a distillation pipeline that went from $60 to $6 per cycle while getting 40% more accurate, and 19 failed training runs that each taught me something the documentation never mentioned. I focus on ML pipelines, full-stack systems with local-first principles, and getting GPU training to work on AMD hardware — which, if you know ROCm, you know is its own adventure.

I'm looking for systems and ML engineering roles in Kuwait's energy sector, where AI meets industrial operations and the problems are actually interesting.
